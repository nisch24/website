# Projects

A deeper look at selected research and engineering work.

## Shape-Constrained Volatility Forecasting

**Domain:** Quantitative Finance · Time-Series Analysis

Led statistical research comparing EWMA, ARIMA, and GARCH models with
shape-constrained regularization to improve multi-horizon financial forecasts.
The core insight: imposing monotonicity and convexity constraints on variance
estimators produces more stable out-of-sample predictions without sacrificing
flexibility.

**Tools:** R, rugarch, forecast

## Red Cross Donor Engagement Analytics

**Domain:** Health Analytics · Machine Learning

As part of the Nittany AI Advance program, I partnered with the American Red
Cross to optimize national blood drive locations. We identified 15+ public
datasets, engineered geospatial features, and benchmarked CNN, RNN, and XGBoost
models achieving ~80% classification accuracy. Final recommendations were
presented to national stakeholders.

**Tools:** PyTorch, Scikit-learn, Pandas, GeoPandas

## Autonomous Vehicle Simulation Stack

**Domain:** Robotics · Sensor Fusion · Systems Engineering

Built software-in-the-loop (SIL) and hardware-in-the-loop (HIL) simulation
environments for Penn State's Level IV autonomous vehicle research team.
Integrated sensor fusion pipelines spanning LiDAR, camera, and IMU data across
AWSIM, CARLA, and ROS frameworks.

**Tools:** AWSIM, CARLA, ROS, C++, Python

## Predictive Credit Union Intelligence

**Domain:** Financial Analytics · Cloud Infrastructure

Automated cross-environment data validation with Python and deployed predictive
savings models from 300K+ member records. Outcomes were visualized in PowerBI
dashboards for executive decision-making.

**Tools:** Python, SQL, Azure Data Factory, PowerBI
